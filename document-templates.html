S Definition of Done (DoD) Scrum Guide — Sprint 0 / Ongoing

The DoD is the team's quality contract. Every story must pass ALL DoD criteria before it can be marked Done. Teams should evolve the DoD based on retrospective feedback.

H Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM) PMI PMBOK — Planning / Execution

Traces every business requirement through to user stories, test cases, and delivery status. If it's not in the RTM, it's not in scope. Prevents gold-plating and scope creep.
