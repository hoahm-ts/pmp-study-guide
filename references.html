Reference Materials & Study Bibliography

Tài liệu tham khảo & Thư mục tài liệu học tập

All primary references behind this study guide — organized by category. These are the books, official standards, and question banks that form the foundation of PMP and Agile certification preparation.

📘 PMI Official Standards / Tiêu chuẩn chính thức PMI

🇺🇸 English

These are the authoritative sources that PMP exam questions are directly drawn from. The ECO (Examination Content Outline) defines the exam domains; PMBOK 7 defines the principles and performance domains tested.

🇻🇳 Tiếng Việt

Đây là các nguồn có thẩm quyền mà câu hỏi thi PMP được rút ra trực tiếp. ECO (Examination Content Outline) định nghĩa các domain của kỳ thi; PMBOK 7 định nghĩa các nguyên tắc và performance domains được kiểm tra.

TitleAuthor / PublisherYearExam RelevanceNotes
PMBOK Guide — 7th Edition
(A Guide to the Project Management Body of Knowledge)		 Project Management Institute (PMI) 2021 HIGH — Primary reference Principles-based. Covers 12 principles + 8 performance domains. The current standard for PMP exam. Available in Vietnamese translation.
PMBOK Guide — 6th Edition
(A Guide to the Project Management Body of Knowledge)		 Project Management Institute (PMI) 2017 MEDIUM — Legacy context Process-based (49 processes, 10 knowledge areas). Still referenced for predictive/Waterfall process questions. Useful for understanding EVM, scope management, and procurement processes.
PMP Examination Content Outline (ECO) Project Management Institute (PMI) 2021 HIGH — Exam blueprint Free from PMI website. Defines the 3 domains (People 42%, Process 50%, Business Environment 8%) and all 35 tasks. Must-read before starting exam prep.
Agile Practice Guide PMI + Agile Alliance 2017 HIGH — Agile portion 50% of PMP exam is agile/hybrid. This guide covers agile mindset, Scrum, Kanban, XP, and hybrid approaches. Free for PMI members.
📌
What the PMP Exam Actually Tests

The PMP exam does not test PMBOK 6 processes by name. It tests your ability to make the right PM decision in a scenario — which draws from both PMBOK 7 principles and real agile practice. Read PMBOK 7 for the mindset; use Rita's book for scenario practice.

🏃 Scrum & Agile Framework Guides

TitleAuthorYearExam RelevanceNotes
Scrum Guide 2020
(The Definitive Guide to Scrum)		 Ken Schwaber & Jeff Sutherland 2020 HIGH Available in English and Vietnamese. Only 13 pages — read it in full. Defines Scrum roles (PO, SM, Developers), events (5 ceremonies), and artifacts (3 + commitments). Free at scrumguides.org. The 2020 revision removed prescriptive elements (no more "sprint" velocity as a metric).

(Bản tiếng Việt)		 Ken Schwaber & Jeff Sutherland (dịch) 2020 HIGH Phiên bản tiếng Việt chính thức. Hữu ích để ghi nhớ thuật ngữ song ngữ khi làm bài thi. Nên đọc song song cả hai phiên bản.
Nexus Guide v1.1
(The Definitive Guide to Scaling Scrum with Nexus)		 Ken Schwaber / Scrum.org 2018 MEDIUM Available in English and Vietnamese. Covers scaling Scrum across 3–9 teams using one Product Backlog. Relevant for SAFe/LeSS questions. Introduces the Nexus Integration Team and cross-team dependency management. Free at scrum.org.
Agile Project Management For Dummies Mark C. Layton, Steven J. Ostermiller, Dean J. Kynaston 2020 MEDIUM Accessible introduction to agile PM concepts. Good for building intuition on agile mindset, servant leadership, and iterative delivery before diving into the more dense PMI materials. Covers Scrum, Kanban, XP, and hybrid approaches in practical terms.

📝 Practice Question Banks / Ngân hàng câu hỏi luyện thi

🇺🇸 English

Practice questions are the most important study activity once you have learned the concepts. Aim for 200+ questions per week in the final 4 weeks before your exam. Focus on understanding why wrong answers are wrong, not just memorizing right answers.

🇻🇳 Tiếng Việt

Câu hỏi thực hành là hoạt động học tập quan trọng nhất sau khi đã nắm vững các khái niệm. Mục tiêu 200+ câu/tuần trong 4 tuần cuối trước kỳ thi. Tập trung vào việc hiểu tại sao đáp án sai lại sai, không chỉ ghi nhớ đáp án đúng.

ResourceFormatCoverageHow to Use
Bộ đề PMP 2025 Excel (.xlsx) — Vietnamese PMP ECO 2021 format Use as timed mock exam sessions. Simulate real exam conditions: 180 questions, 230 minutes, no interruptions. Track score by domain to find weak areas.
Bộ đề Scrum PSM I Word (.docx) — Vietnamese Scrum Guide 2020 / PSM I level Work through before studying Scrum sections. PSM I questions reinforce Scrum fundamentals at a higher precision than PMP questions. Good for locking in Scrum role and event definitions.
Đề Agile 2025 (Scrum 2) Word (.docx) — Vietnamese Agile / Scrum advanced scenarios Use after completing Scrum basics. Covers Kanban, Lean, hybrid scenarios. Pairs well with Mike Griffiths' PMI-ACP book for concept reinforcement.
PMI-ACP Exam Prep: Questions, Answers & Explanations PDF — English PMI-ACP format (agile focus) 600+ practice questions with detailed explanations. Use for the agile half of PMP prep. Explanations are the most valuable part — read every explanation, not just right/wrong scores.
Rita Mulcahy — Chapter-end Questions PDF (embedded in Rita's book) PMP ECO — all domains ~1,600 questions across the book. Do chapter questions immediately after reading each chapter while concepts are fresh. Revisit missed questions in the final review week.

📌
The Most Important Rule for PMP Prep

When you read a scenario question, ask: "What would a servant-leader, stakeholder-focused, proactive PM do?" The PMP exam always rewards proactive communication, early escalation, respect for process (but not bureaucracy), and team empowerment. When in doubt, the answer that protects the project, the team, and the stakeholder relationship simultaneously is usually correct.
